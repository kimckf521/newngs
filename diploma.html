What is Online Diploma Program?

The Online Diploma Program is designed to meet the need of students who are not able commit to fix location study but still requires a high-school diploma at the end of the full-course study.

The student opted this route will be enrolled in an accredited school and completed all subject learning and formative assessment at NGS Online School. Upon meeting the course completion requirement, the student will take the official assessments at the physical location of the school. Those who meet the graduation assessment criteria of the school will then be issued a credible high-school diploma and full school transcript.
